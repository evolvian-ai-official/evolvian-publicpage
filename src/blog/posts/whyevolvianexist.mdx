## FAQ

### Who is Evolvian for?

Evolvian is designed for teams that need to scale support, improve lead
qualification, and keep response quality consistent across channels.

### Do I need a technical team to use Evolvian?

No. Most teams can launch with minimal technical support using built-in setup
flows and widget configuration options.

### Can Evolvian work with my existing business documents?

Yes. You can upload documents and context so the assistant responds based on
your own information and business rules.
