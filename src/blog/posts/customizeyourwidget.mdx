## FAQ

### Which settings have the biggest visual impact first?

Header color, message bubble colors, and font style usually create the strongest
brand alignment quickly.

### Can I customize the widget without coding?

Yes. Most customization options are available directly in your dashboard under
Customize Widget.

### Can I enforce legal consent in the widget?

Yes. You can enable legal links and consent requirements based on your policy
needs and user flow.
